About Grapevine 2025

Grapevine is a one-day event designed to empower and equip high school students with the skills, inspiration, and connections they need to become tomorrow's leaders.

Featuring industry speakers, hands-on workshops, leadership training, and real-world projects, the conference creates a platform for students to challenge themselves and grow.

Whether you're passionate about technology, entrepreneurship, art, or social change, Grapevine has something for you.
