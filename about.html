About Us

We've been in your shoes - we can help you find your path

Our Mission

Empowering youths with the tools, perspectives, and support needed to navigate their post-secondary paths and contribute positively to their communities.

Who We Are

OYGN is a youth-led, nonprofit organization focused on empowering high school students across Ontario through career exploration, university prep, and leadership growth. We’ve been where you are - and we’re here to help you take the next step with confidence.

How We Can Help

From career workshops to university application support, we will give you tools, insights, and connections to plan your future. Our events are built to inspire and guide you - through real stories, practical resources, and a community that believes in your potential.

Our Values

Empowerment

We equip youth with tools, knowledge, and confidence to take charge of their future and define success on their own terms.

Communinty

We build spaces where students, mentors, and educators come together to support, uplift, and grow alongside each other.

Relatability

Our conferences are led by students and young professionals who truly understand your journey - because they’ve lived it too.

Accessibility

We believe every student, regardless of background, deserves access to meaningful career and education guidance.
